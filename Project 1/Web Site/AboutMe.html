About Me

Life for me has always been about making the best out of what life gives you, which is why I appreciate working out, traveling, and learning about technology.

Technology for me has been my passion since I was ten years old and started with an old HP computer that by today's standards would be considered a dinosaur. Ever since, I have taught myself how to troubleshoot and diagnose computer hardware and software issues efficiently. I have built many computers for my family, friends, and of course myself.

Many years later, I began learning about the expanse world of databases via an internship at Bankers Healthcare Group (BHG). From there I gained experience and knowledge in SQL Server, the SQL language, and reporting & integration services. After months of hard work, I was accepted as a full time employee and have been working at BHG for over two years now.

Technology never stops expanding, which is why keeping up on the latest trends is absolutely crucial.
