Chicken & Bean Chili

Have you ever craved a delicious, hot, and spicy chili dish, but could not find the perfect recipe?
If so, then look no further! This recipe will become your new zesty favorite!

This dish serves around five people.
Estimated cooking time: One Hour.

This recipe calls for the following ingredients:
  • 1 can of spicy chili beans
  • 2 lbs of chicken cut into pieces, not too small though!
  • 2 or 3 hot peppers cut into small pieces
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 2 carrots
  • Hot chili seasoning, you can usually find this for 99 cents in a grocery store
  • 1 can of tomato sauce for the chili base
  • 1 chopped tomato

Follow the following steps to create your perfect chili:

  1. Prepare your ingredients: have your vegetables chopping into smaller sizes, prepare beans, etc.
  2. Throw all your ingredients into your slow cooker of choice, including the seasoning.
  3. Fill the slow cooker with water till the water reaches the top of the ingredients
  4. Turn the slow cooker on high and leave for around four or five hours, the longer you cook it, the more tender the chicken will be!
  5. Chicken and vegetables should be tender, if they're still tough let the ingredients cook longer.
